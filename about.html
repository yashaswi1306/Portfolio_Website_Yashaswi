About Me

Hello! I'm Yashaswi Priya. I am a first year Dual Degree student studying Computer Science at IIIT Hyderabad. I wanted to get into engineering, less due to a love for coding and more so for my love of mathematics. More so than the coding aspect, I like getting into the 'saar' of a question, figuring out what connection can be made between seemingly unrelated entities and how mathematical models can be implemented for the same. I opted for CLD as my top preference, because I was simply fascinated by what they were doing here at IIITH in the field of computational linguistics. I remember researching about the IIITH labs and always circling back to how cool LTRC was. I spent most of my vacation reading articles and watching videos to learn more about linguistics and cl, as I had very little prior knowledge of the same. And now that I'm here, I am really glad I chose this as my course. I'm a classically trained Bharatnatyam dancer, which has taught me discipline and perseverance. I enjoy reading books (mostly modern-classics or crime thrillers) and painting in my free time. I am a huge animal lover and absolutely adore dogs, especially the campus canines.
